## Usage Instructions

1. Complete this template at the end of each study week (Friday or Sunday).
2. Gather all structured notes from the week before starting.
3. Write the Week at a Glance section first, from memory.
4. Identify themes by looking for recurring concepts across sources.
5. Map connections using your concept map from the week.
6. Be honest about your understanding strength — this is for your benefit, not for marking.
7. Set specific, actionable items for next week based on the gaps you identified.
